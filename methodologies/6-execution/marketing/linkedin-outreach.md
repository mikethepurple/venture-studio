# LinkedIn Outreach

## When to use

Runs in step 6 execution. Produces a complete LinkedIn outreach campaign for B2B validation — target profiles, connection request copy, follow-up sequence, and tracking template.

## Inputs needed

- brief.md
- step-4/decisions.md (business model, value prop)
- Any landing page or product URL from the current iteration's build output

## Process

1. **Define target profiles.** Based on the business model and value prop:
   - **Ideal Customer Profile:** Job titles, company size, industry, seniority level
   - **Search filters:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator filters (or regular LinkedIn search terms)
   - **Volume target:** 50-100 connection requests per week
   - **Exclusions:** Who to NOT target (too senior, wrong department, competitors)

2. **Write connection request copy** (300 character limit):
   - No pitch in the connection request — just relevance and curiosity
   - Reference something specific: their role, company, or a shared interest
   - Write 3 variations to A/B test

3. **Write 3-message follow-up sequence:**
   - **Message 1 (Day 1 after acceptance):** Thank + one-sentence value prop + question. No link yet.
   - **Message 2 (Day 3):** Share a relevant insight or resource. Mention the product naturally. Include link.
   - **Message 3 (Day 7):** Direct ask — "Would you be open to a 15-min demo?" or "Can I send you early access?"

4. **Write demo/pitch message** for warm leads who respond positively:
   - 3-4 sentences: problem → solution → ask
   - Include the product URL
   - Specific CTA (book a call, try the product, give feedback)

5. **Response rate tracking template:**
   - Connections sent / accepted / Message 1 sent / replied / Message 2 sent / replied / demos booked
   - Expected benchmarks: 30-40% acceptance, 15-25% reply to M1, 5-10% demo conversion
