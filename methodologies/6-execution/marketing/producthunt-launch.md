# Product Hunt Launch

## When to use

Runs in step 6 execution. Produces a complete Product Hunt launch plan — listing copy, launch-day strategy, pre-launch community prep, and engagement plan.

## Inputs needed

- brief.md
- step-4/decisions.md (business model, value prop)
- Any landing page or product URL from the current iteration's build output

## Process

1. **Write the Product Hunt listing:**
   - **Tagline** (60 chars max): Clear, benefit-driven, no jargon
   - **Description** (260 chars): What it does + who it's for + key differentiator
   - **Topics:** 3-5 relevant PH topics/tags
   - **Gallery:** Describe 3-5 images/GIFs to prepare (hero screenshot, feature highlights, before/after)

2. **Write the first comment (maker story):**
   - Why you built this (personal motivation, problem you faced)
   - What makes it different from existing solutions
   - What's the current state (MVP, beta, launched)
   - Specific ask: "I'd love feedback on [specific thing]"
   - Keep it authentic, not salesy — PH community values transparency

3. **Launch-day timing and prep:**
   - Launch at 12:01 AM PT (start of the PH day)
   - Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
   - Avoid major product launches (check PH upcoming page)
   - Have the product page live and tested before launch

4. **Pre-launch community notifications:**
   - List communities from earlier research where launch can be shared
   - Write a non-spammy announcement post for each community type (Reddit, Discord, Twitter, newsletter)
   - Emphasis: share the launch, don't ask for upvotes (against PH rules)

5. **Engagement strategy for launch day:**
   - Respond to every comment within 30 minutes
   - Thank supporters individually
   - Answer questions with substance, not canned responses
   - Share progress updates throughout the day
   - Cross-post milestones to Twitter/X

| Metric | Low | Good | Strong |
|---|---|---|---|

| Comments | <10 | 20-50 | >100 |
| Website visits | <200 | 500-1000 | >2000 |
| Signups | <20 | 50-150 | >300 |
