# GTM Strategy

<!-- model: opus -->

## When to use

Always runs in step 4 (wave 1). Designs the go-to-market strategy including channel strategy, launch sequence, partnerships, and growth loops for each solution.

## Inputs needed

- brief.md
- step-3/decisions.md
- step-3/{item-slug}/competitor-mapping.md
- step-3/{item-slug}/scout-enrichment.md
- step-3/{item-slug}/ad-reach-sizing.md
- step-3/{item-slug}/opportunity-ranking.md
- step-3/synthesis.md

## Process

1. **Read all inputs.** Understand the solution, its target audience, competitive landscape, and addressable reach from ad-reach-sizing.md. Note which channels competitors use successfully (from competitor-mapping.md and scout-enrichment.md).

2. **Define target customer segments.** For this solution:
   - Primary segment (first 1,000 users) — who, where, what motivates them
   - Secondary segment (users 1K-10K) — expansion audience
   - Long-term segment (10K+) — mass market characteristics
   - For CIS markets: specify country priority (Russia, Kazakhstan, Georgia, Armenia, Uzbekistan, etc.)

3. **Design channel strategy.** For each acquisition channel:
   - **Telegram:** Mini App distribution, channel/group seeding, bot virality, TON ecosystem integration
   - **Social media:** VK, Instagram, YouTube (Russia/CIS specific), Twitter/X (crypto community)
   - **Crypto-native:** DeFi aggregators, crypto Telegram groups, TON ecosystem, airdrop/incentive programs
   - **Content/SEO:** Russian-language content strategy, keyword targeting
   - **Partnerships:** Telegram channels, crypto influencers, local media, fintech partners
   - **Paid:** Telegram Ads, VK Ads, Google Ads (where available), crypto ad networks
   - For each channel: estimated CAC, volume potential, time to scale, CIS-specific considerations

4. **Design launch sequence.** Phase the GTM into 3 stages:
   - **Phase 1 (Month 1-3): Seed** — manual, high-touch acquisition. Community building. First 500-1000 users. What's the hook?
   - **Phase 2 (Month 4-8): Growth** — turn on paid channels, activate partnerships, launch referral program. Target 1K-10K users.
   - **Phase 3 (Month 9-12): Scale** — optimize channels, expand geographically, add growth loops. Target 10K-50K users.
   - For each phase: specific tactics, budget allocation, success metrics

5. **Design growth loops.** Identify 2-3 self-reinforcing growth mechanisms:
   - Viral loops (sharing, referrals, social proof)
   - Content loops (user-generated content, predictions as shareable content)
   - Network effects (more users = better product)
   - Data loops (more usage = better predictions/recommendations)
   - Ecosystem loops (TON/Telegram integration advantages)

6. **Identify GTM risks.** Top 3-5 risks specific to this solution's GTM:
   - Platform dependency (Telegram policy changes)
   - Regulatory barriers by country
   - Payment friction (USDT/TON onboarding complexity)
   - Competitor response (incumbents copying features)
   - Cultural/language barriers across CIS
