# Trend Scanning

## When to use

Always runs in step 3. Identifies technology, cultural, regulatory, and economic trends that create tailwinds or headwinds for the market being analyzed.

## Inputs needed

- brief.md
- step-2/decisions.md

## Process

1. **Technology trends:** Search for AI/ML, infrastructure, platform shifts, and cost collapses relevant to this market. Look for capabilities that make previously impossible solutions now feasible.

2. **Cultural/demographic trends:** Search for generational shifts, work pattern changes, and values shifts (sustainability, privacy, authenticity) that affect demand in this market.

3. **Regulatory trends:** Search for new compliance requirements, deregulation, or government programs that create forced buyers or open new markets.

4. **Economic trends:** Search for cost pressures, labor changes, or economic shifts that make this market more or less attractive.

5. **Rate each trend:**
   - Velocity: Fast (months) / Medium (years) / Slow (decades)
   - Reversibility: Low (one-way ratchet) / High (could reverse)
   - Enablement: Does this make a new solution *possible* or just make old solutions *better*?

6. **Find intersections:** Identify where 2+ trends converge to create a specific opportunity window in this market.

## Output format

```markdown
# Trend Scanning — [Market Name]

*Date: [today]*

## Relevant Trends

| Trend | Category | Velocity | Reversibility | Enablement |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [name] | Tech/Cultural/Regulatory/Economic | Fast/Medium/Slow | Low/High | New/Better |

## Trend Details

### [Trend Name]
**What's happening:** [2-3 sentences with data]
**Source:** [URL]
**Implication for this market:** [how it affects the opportunity]

## Trend Intersections

### [Intersection Name]
**Trends:** [Trend A] + [Trend B]
**Opportunity:** [what product/service becomes possible at this intersection]
**Why now:** [why this matters today vs. 2 years ago]

## Risk Assessment
- What could slow or reverse these trends?
```
